# Product Requirements Prompt (PRP) — ✅ Completed

## 1. Project Context & Objectives
**BUG FIX & UI REFINEMENT:** The Octave Knob was incorrectly placed inside a full-width row above the keyboard, causing extreme right-alignment and excessive vertical margin on the Input card. 

**Objective:** 1. Remove the full-width control row.
2. Anchor the Octave Knob to the exact horizontal center of the cyan "PLAY/START NOTE" banner, floating immediately above it.
3. Reduce the excessive top margin of the main keyboard wrapper to tighten up the card's visual rhythm.

## 2. Technical Decisions & Dependencies
* **Absolute Positioning:** To float the knob above a specific banner without breaking the flex-row alignment of the other banners, the "PLAY/START NOTE" banner must have `position: 'relative'` and `overflow: 'visible'`. The Octave Knob container will be placed inside it with `position: 'absolute', top: '-40px', left: '50%', transform: 'translateX(-50%)'`.
* **Margin Reduction:** The main wrapper's `marginTop` (which was previously increased to accommodate the bad row layout) must be reduced to just enough pixels to fit the absolute-positioned knob (e.g., `mt-4` or `marginTop: '16px'`).
