# Product Requirements Prompt (PRP) — ✅ Completed

## 1. Project Context & Objectives
**UI Refinement:** The 49-key layout and zone banners are correctly aligned, but the "PLAY/START NOTE" cyan banner is too cramped, causing the text to wrap awkwardly. Additionally, the spacing between the main "Input" card header and the keyboard assembly is too tight.

**Objective:** 1. Increase the top margin/padding above the zone banners to give the keyboard assembly more breathing room from the card header.
2. Extract the Octave Knob and Play/Start settings cog from inside the cyan "PLAY/START NOTE" banner and relocate them directly *above* that specific banner, aligning them to the right.

## 2. Technical Decisions & Dependencies
* **Layout Structure:** Moving the controls out of the cyan banner means they need a new home. To keep them visually associated with the Play/Start zone without breaking the dynamic width math of the banners, we will place them in a container right-aligned *above* the entire zone banner row. 
* **Spacing:** A simple `marginTop` or `gap` adjustment on the main wrapper inside `KeySplitKeyboard.tsx` (or its parent container) will solve the breathing room issue.
